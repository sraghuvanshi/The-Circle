    Closed community of the most driven, humble and informed entrepreneurial talent.

    what is the circle?

    the circle is a curated invite-only community of go-getters who are either in the early stages of their startup journey or plan to start in near future, and understand the fundamentals of getting into a startup.

    the circle's mission

    we realised there isn't a platform for 'entrepreneurial talent' to discuss & brainstorm ideas, get each other's input on their early stage business model/product, as well as get guidance from industry veterans and entrepreneurs.


    hence, we decided to build a community of to-be entrepreneurs to enable them to kickstart their journey. It could be about finding co-founders, doing market research, developing a unique insight, understanding their industry deeply, getting a sound advisory board, raising seed capital or something specific to their domain.

    the circle meetups

    the circle meetups are closed 15 member brainstorming sessions over dinner, that happen twice a month, on every alternate weekend.

    who should attend?

    individuals who are in the early stages of their startup.

    individuals who plan to start in the near future.

    individuals who are inclined to work with startups before starting one.

    invitation process

    to ensure a fruitful networking and learning experience for all members, a preliminary screening is done on the above parameters.
